Why Now?


The next decade is going to be instrumental in determining our way of life. If pollution levels remain constant for the next 20 years we will be over the 2Â°C outer limit set in the Paris Agreement which would mean catastrophic pollution levels.

Fortunately we have a solution which is the Off Fossil Fuels Act which transitions the energy economy to 100% renewable energy by 2035. Now is the time for every candidate to choose between dirty energy like fracking and coal or the life of the people and planet. We have no time to waste. It is up to us to transition to a clean energy future. THIS IS AN EMERGENCY. We can stop it. Let's make it happen.

The No Fossil Fuel money Pledge

Dirty fossil fuel money is corrupting our political system and blocking bold action on climate change. We need leaders that reject the influence of fossil fuel money and plan for a just, climate-safe, renewable energy future for everyone, not just the few.

Taking the pledge means that a candidateâs campaign will adopt a policy to not knowingly accept any contributions over $200 from the PACs, executives, or front groups of fossil fuel companies â companies whose primary business is the extraction, processing, distribution, or sale of oil, gas, or coal.

The #OFFAct Pledge

A pledge to be a leader on climate change issues and support efforts in the community to move us off fossil fuels and achieve a just transition to 100% clean, renewable energy and to oppose any project that will expand fossil fuel production, and its use or transport, and work to quickly phase out existing fossil fuel infrastructure in my jurisdiction.
